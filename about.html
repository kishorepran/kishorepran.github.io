Who I Am

A few fun facts

I am a hardware and gadget enthusiast guy. When I am working at a stretch, I love to listen music and grab a cup of coffee. When I am drained out I go for subway to refill and a movie show in theatre follows after.

A look into my life

My regular day starts with an early morning run followed by a swim at near by lake. The day ends with a casual read om emerging trends in technology. Some times I also read off the track like articles on HR, finance and business start up stories.
At weekends I teach Mathematics & Physics to students of 11th and 12th grade. Not only it gave me new ideas and challenging problems but also staying in touch with science has helped me solve many problems that would not have been possible other wise.
Throughout the years I've worked for places that feel the need to constantly burnout their employees with different requests and difficult timelines. Having been put through many experiences in my past, I had some great ones that allowed me to work with some amazing people who have played key roles in my career. I have worked very hard to get where I am today, but I'm just as passionate about where my career is leading me. The challenges that I deal with on a day-to-day basis I find meaningful and rewarding. I love what I do and I will do whatever it takes me to explore further in my career.

Just another day working at

ATOS Kolkata

An early start

As smartphones have become more commonplace and less for the gadget fans alone, apps have evolved from being game heavy to being useful and consumer oriented these days. Well I started off everyday with a breakfast that kept changing but always included a mug of coffee.
I specialise in iOS development, it really helps with platform knowledge to also be an end user. One of the great things about app development is that team sizes tend to be fairly small, so early on everyone gets a chance to have some input into how the app is going to turn out. App teams tend to be small which means the role of a developer typically spans requirements, software development, testing as well as facing clients .This variety and influence at all stages of the project really appeals to me.As we all know that Quality comes first so to make sure of this I always checked that the quality of apps steadily keeps improving across the board. In order to explore more the journey here is amazingly continuing with lot of fun.
