A brief background

I started my carrer as a J2EE developer, but when I was introduced to iOS, I was just amazed at the beauty of Objective C, the power of Xcode, and the well-managed documentation. I have continuously thrived since then, and writing cleaner, more efficient, and better manageable code became my passion. Today, with over 8+ years of experience as a mobile application developer focused on iOS, I'm a DevOps and Design enthusiast. I've had the privilege of working on design award-winning apps that have been downloaded over a million times from the app store. As an AWS Business professional, I'm also keen on cloud and server-side programming. At my leisure, I love watching movies, listening to music, and at times going for a swim - especially in the monsoons.
